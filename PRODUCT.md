# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

ios

## Users

Renlyst is for pharmacy students and early-career trainees learning during community-pharmacy shifts. They often use the app one-handed, between shelf tasks, with limited attention and mixed English/Arabic study material. Their primary job is to turn a medicine they saw into a durable, connected memory without confusing local brands with the active drug.

## Product Purpose

Renlyst is an offline-first personal learning companion. One active-ingredient profile owns the clinical learning information; many local brand products can attach their own names, strengths, package photos, and shelf details. Success means the learner can add what they saw quickly, return to the right fact, practice it in a short session, and preserve a trustworthy history of their progress.

## Positioning

The calmest way to connect pharmacy-shelf experience to one reusable active-drug knowledge profile.

## Brand Personality

Calm, intelligent, and encouraging. Renlyst speaks directly, uses concrete verbs, and treats progress as a steady learning rhythm rather than a loud game. English leads navigation; concise Arabic supports learning content where it adds value.

## Anti-references

Renlyst must not resemble a generated SaaS dashboard, a purple AI assistant, a dense hospital information system, a crystal-themed game, or a collection of decorative cards. It avoids glossy medical clichés, competing primary actions, hidden destructive behavior, and AI as the default path.

## Design Principles

- One clear next action beats a menu of equal choices.
- Active ingredients are stable knowledge; brands are attached product evidence.
- Familiar iOS behavior creates confidence and speed.
- Show provenance, consequences, and empty states honestly.
- Reward repeated recall with calm progress, not noise.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

The app supports Dynamic Type, VoiceOver, system contrast, Dark Mode, Reduce Motion, minimum 44-point targets, and right-to-left Arabic content blocks. Meaning never depends on color, motion, or imagery alone.
